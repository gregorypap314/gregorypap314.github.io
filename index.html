About

Currently studying a bachelor in Aerospace Engineering (Hons) with a minor in Nuclear Engineering, I have undertaken several projects of varying scales to apply my learnings. Over the past three years I have built satellite payloads, telecommunications ground stations, lunar rovers, high-altitude balloons and more recently a hyperspectral imager for small-satellites. This experience has enabled me to lead student teams of up to one-hundred engineers, building soft leadership skills to complement my technical experience.
